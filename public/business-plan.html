1. Executive Summary

GreyhoundIQ is a modern, mobile-first Australian greyhound racing data platform that aggregates national race data into a single, beautifully designed interface. It replaces the fragmented, outdated, and overpriced tools that currently serve this market.

🎯 The Opportunity

The Australian greyhound racing industry turns over $4.3 billion in annual wagering across ~500,000 active punters. Yet the data tools serving this audience are stuck in 2010 — user-contributed, ad-heavy, GBP-priced, and fragmented across 6+ state body websites. Nobody offers AI predictions, unified national data, or a decent mobile experience.

7. Operational Plan

Lean operation — one developer (AI-assisted), automated data pipelines, near-zero infrastructure management.

🔄

Data Pipeline

Automated Python workers run on cron. Betfair CSVs seed 5 years of history. TAB API provides daily live race fields. FastTrack (VIC) and Tasracing scraped for stats/breeding. Community contributions fill gaps.

🏗️

Infrastructure

Single Hetzner VPS (4 vCPU, 8GB RAM, ~$8/mo). Next.js app + PostgreSQL + Redis all on one box. Migrate to managed Postgres only when concurrent users exceed ~500. Cloudflare in front for CDN/DDoS protection.

👨‍💻

Team

Solo developer (Daniel) using Hermes Agent + AI-assisted development for 10x output. No hires needed until Year 2. Then: 1 part-time data engineer for pipeline maintenance, 1 part-time content writer for SEO.

📊

Monitoring & Quality

Automated health checks on ingestion pipeline. Data accuracy dashboard comparing our results against official sources. User feedback/report system for data discrepancies. Sentry for error tracking.

8. Risk Analysis & Mitigation

✓ Key Strengths

  • Near-zero infrastructure cost (~$14/mo) means profitability from first paying customer
  • Multiple free data sources confirmed — no single-source dependency
  • Competitor is entrenched in outdated tech and unlikely to modernise quickly
  • 97% gross margin — subscription model with zero marginal cost per user
  • AI predictions and GPS data are moats that competitors can't easily replicate
  • Australian-specific focus builds local trust and community

⚠ Key Risks

  • Data partnerships could be expensive or denied — mitigated by free scraping fallbacks
  • TAB API or scraped sites could change structure — mitigated by multi-source strategy
  • Racing authorities may object to scraping — mitigated by only using publicly accessible data and pivoting to partnerships
  • greyhound-data.com could modernise in response — mitigated by AI features they can't replicate and AU-specific focus
  • Responsible gambling compliance requirements — must implement 18+ checks and Gamble Responsibly messaging
  • Seasonal user behaviour — racing is year-round but wagering peaks spring carnival

9. Target Audience

Three primary personas drive GreyhoundIQ's marketing strategy. Each has distinct needs, channels, and willingness to pay.

📊 "The Serious Punter" — Primary (60% of revenue)

Male, 35–65 · Bets $200–$2,000/week · Values data over tips

Bets on greyhounds 3–5 days a week. Currently uses a mix of free sites, Punters.com.au, and maybe a greyhound-data.com subscription. Frustrated by fragmented data, outdated UIs, and the lack of predictions. Wants speed maps, sectional times, box bias stats, and AI predictions to find an edge. Will pay $12–$29/month for a genuine advantage. This is the core Pro and Pro+ subscriber.

Primary target Pro / Pro+ tier LTV: $300–$600/yr

🐕 "The Breeder/Trainer" — Secondary (25% of revenue)

Mixed demographics · Industry participant · Values pedigree data

Active in the breeding/training side of the industry. Needs pedigree databases, testmating tools, sire statistics, litter performance tracking. Currently relies on greyhound-data.com (unreliable, user-contributed) and FastTrack (VIC only). Will pay for accurate, national breeding data with modern tools. This persona values depth and accuracy over speed/predictions.

Secondary target Pro tier LTV: $99–$249/yr

📱 "The Casual Punter" — Tertiary (15% of revenue, 70% of users)

Broad demographics · Bets occasionally · Values simplicity

Bets on greyhounds occasionally — maybe during big races or when at the pub. Checks form on their phone before a bet. Currently uses free sites or just bets on vibes. Won't pay for data but will use the free tier and convert to Pro if they get serious. This persona is the top of the funnel — they drive SEO traffic and free-to-paid conversion.

Funnel driver Free → Pro conversion LTV: $0 → $99/yr

12. Content & SEO Strategy

SEO is the primary growth engine. Racing data is highly searchable — every dog name, every track, every sire is a keyword. Build pages for all of them.

🔍

Programmatic SEO

Every dog in the database gets a SEO-optimised profile page (e.g. /dogs/aston-queen). Every track gets a stats page (e.g. /tracks/wentworth-park/box-stats). Every sire gets a progeny page. This creates 10,000+ indexable pages targeting long-tail keywords with high intent.

📝

Content Hub

Publish 2–3 articles/week targeting informational keywords: "best greyhound racing tips", "how to read greyhound form", "Wentworth Park box bias guide", "top sires in Australia 2026". Each article links back to relevant data pages, driving conversion to Pro.

14. Partnership Strategy

🤝

Tipster Partnerships

Partner with greyhound racing tipsters on Twitter and YouTube. Offer them free Pro+ accounts in exchange for promoting GreyhoundIQ to their audience. Revenue share model: 20% recurring commission for any subscriber they refer.

🎙️

Podcast Sponsorships

Sponsor 2–3 Australian racing podcasts (e.g. racing industry podcasts). Negotiate promo code deals — listeners get 20% off, podcast gets revenue share. Target: ~5,000 downloads/episode podcasts.

📱

App/Tool Integrations

Offer API access to other racing tools, betting calculators, and tipster platforms. Pro+ API tier creates a B2B revenue stream and positions GreyhoundIQ as the data backbone for the ecosystem.

🏆

Industry Body Relationships

Build relationships with GRV, GRNSW, and Racing Queensland. Position GreyhoundIQ as a partner that grows the sport by improving the punter experience. Explore co-marketing opportunities and official data licensing.
